SemanticBrowser.org

A D3js-based-browser on a filtered version of the Semantic Scholar corpus.
The user can search for papers and build its own cluster of interesting papers including in and out-citations.
Above the paper graph the one referred to the authors is displayed.
With this tool we hope to simplify the process of choicing reviewers that know well a subject but are not conflicted with the authors of the paper under scrutiny.

Usage:

  1. build a topic-based graph by searching for papers titles in the AREA PAPER searchbar
  2. build the set of conflicting authors by searching their names in the CONFILICTING AUTHORS search bar
Once the graph and the conflicting authors list have been populated, good reviewer candidates are shown in the author area.
For further information visit the GitHub page.
